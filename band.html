About the band

Linkin Park is an American rock band from Agoura Hills, California.

Formed in 1996, the band rose to international fame with their debut album Hybrid Theory (2000).

Band members include:

  • Rob Bourdon
  • Brad Delson
  • Mike Shinoda
  • Dave Farell
  • Joe Hahn

Past members include:

  • Mark Wakefield
  • Kyle Christner
  • Chester Bennington
